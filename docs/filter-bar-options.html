How It Works

Collapsed: "showing: all — or filter by attention, counselling... [+6 more]"

Expanded: All tags inline in sentence structure with "[less]" toggle

Pros:

  • Most human-readable
  • Clearly explains what filters do
  • Conversational tone
  • Good for first-time visitors

Cons:

  • More verbose / takes horizontal space
  • Less minimalist aesthetic
  • May feel over-explained
