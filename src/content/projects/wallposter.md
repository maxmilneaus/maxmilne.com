## Overview:
Karl is a painter. I am a photographer. Together we brought together our skills to make large scale paste ups. We blew up my photos, wheat pasted them to walls, with consent, and then painted them.

This was the start of me wanting to make my own prints and photography unique and one off, akin those prints that came out of dark rooms. My mother would hand colour her black-and-white prints, and this was a hommage though made modern with A0 plan printers.
