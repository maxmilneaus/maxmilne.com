FAQs

We all have questions. Here are some answers. (T&Cs are below)

FAQs:

WiFi:
Yes we NBN and will provide passwords on the day.

What to do with my garbage?
There is a bin in the studio - any bags left in addition we can handle for $15 a bag

Can we come in earlier or have extra time?
Any additional time on top of your booking is charged at $40 per hour, starting 15 mins past the hour, please check with a studio manager first.

What are the shoot start times?
Either 9am or 1pm on a weekday, or 10am and 2pm on a weekend.

What if we want to stay beyond the normal working hours?
This is possible. Please contact the studio manager with your needs and they will try best to tailor an option for you.

What is equipment included with Studio Brunswick?
Each studio hire includes five poly boards and stands, four c-stands, two trestle table, a hair and makeup table, power extension cables, six weight bags and power boards.

Privacy?
The studio is in its own lockable private part of the building.

How about parking?
Drop off and pick up can be done. Plenty of local all day parking available on the side streets.

Sound?
Studio Brunswick is a collective and has fellow creatives close by, please keep the music level to a talking level. 

T&C

The fine print to keep us all on the same page.

BOOKING
For a date to be locked in exclusively for you the 50% deposit is required upon receipt of the invoice. The total hire fee is due 5 working days before you booking date. No bookings are locked in until your deposit is received.

CANCELLATION/CHANGES FEES
Cancellations/changes within 5 working days before the start time of a booking will incur a 50% cancellation fee. Cancellations made less than 3 working days before the start time of a booking will incur a 100% cancellation fee. This includes changes to your bookings for example changing booking date.  

DAMAGE
Any damage of equipment, building or anything else associated with hire of Studio Brunswick the hirer is liable for repair or replacement cost.

LIABILITY
Each photographer is to present their liability insurance prior to shooting. If they don't have any then the photographer responsible for the shoot is to complete and hand in the Liability Waiver to the studio manager on the day. 

STUDIO OVERTIME
Additional hours are charged at a rate of $40 per hour, starting 15 minutes past the hour.

EARLY STARTS
Starts earlier than our open times, 9am weekdays and 10am weekends, will be charged $40 per hour as overtime. For example, if booking a full day and wish to start at 9am on a Saturday you will be charged the $40 overtime and your hire will be from 9am-6pm.

SOUND
As Studio Brunswick is a collective and has fellow creatives close by, including a wood workshop so please keep this in mind when booking.

CLEANING
The studio is to be returned in the same shape it is found. If the studios are left in a messy condition, a minimum $55 cleaning fee applies. Any damage incurred will be billed directly to the hire client.

RUBBISH
Your rubbish is to leave with you - we can handle it for $15 a bag.

Bookings available 7 days a week. 

Studio Contact hours: 10-11am & 4-5pm weekdays
